
Project: THE LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM FOR JOHN WILLEIT HIGHER
INSTITUTE OF NKONGSAMBA

1.4.1 Overall Objective
To design and implement a Learning Management System (LMS) that improves teaching,
learning, communication, and academic management at John Willeit Higher Institute of
Nkongsamba.
1.4.2 Specific Objective
1. To develop LMS functionalities that provide continuous access to lessons, notes, and
learning materials, ensuring consistent teaching and learning even during lecturer
unavailability.
2. To design and implement LMS features that accurately track, record, and evaluate
students’ academic progress and performance over time.
3. To integrate modern learning tools,such as quizzes, multimedia content, and
discussion forums into the LMS while ensuring the platform remains user-friendly
and accessible for both lecturers and students
